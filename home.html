ABOUT US

Silicon Blaze is a student run project by students from JKUAT, University of Nairobi and Strathmore Business School aimed at inspiring high school students and tutoring them in the field of computer programming. Volunteers (university students) will visit specific schools once in fortnight for a two hour session over the weekend with interested students.
